Layout, art design, and prepress preparation for my friend Andriy Cherednychenko's book "The Last Day."

Through the prism of fictional characters and real events, this book depicts the deep contrast between a happy and successful pre-war life and the incomprehensible tragedy of the events that began on February 24, 2022…

All the stories are illustrated with real photographs of their heroes, stylized as brush drawings, and the color block contains moving photos by talented photographers — Anton Shevelyov, Dmitry Kozatsky, Konstantin Reutsky, Vlada and Konstantin Liberov.
